Welcome to my photography page. Here, you can find a range of photographs shot by myself throughout my years as a media student and beyond. Feel free to browse through the different galleries, each grouped as categories and significant trips I have embarked on. The photos taken during my trip to germany in January 2018 were shot on a Sony A7R DSLR camera, which was very trustingly given to me by my college to capture my experience. Other photographs were taken on my own SLR camera (the Fujifilm Finepix S2000HD), and some photos were even captured on a smart-phone (the Apple iPhone 5s).

The photos are all unedited, so what you see in these photos are exactly true to the real life scenario.
