Hi, my name's Matt and I'm a 16 year old Creative Media student from Lincolnshire, UK. I have studied Creative Media for 3 years now, and along the way I have produced a range of content from web design to photography, and video production to print-based products. On this website you can find examples of work I have produced in my time studying the subject. Although my main 'forte' is video production, I have always enjoyed photography, and so whenever I am out filming, I'm always sure to take any photo opportunities I can.

I often upload my work to social media. My main social media platforms are YouTube and Instagram, but links to all social media can be found in the page's header.
